Physics

Formula for Finding the Range of a Projectile Shot from Ground Level

RANGEFOR

This program is used for finding the x position displacement given the initial velocity of the projectile, degree of projectile launch, assuming y final displacement is 0.

Kinematic Formula for Finding the Initial Velocity of a Projectile

IVELOCIT

This program is used for finding the initial velocity of a projectile in standard gravity given launch degree, the range of x position, and the end y displacement.

Centripetal Motion Derivation for Finding the Altitude of a Satelite in Space

ALTSPACE

This program is used for finding the altitude of an orbitting satellite given the period of the satellite's motion.

Kinematic Formula for Finding the Maximum Y Displacement of a Projectile

YMAX

This program is used for finding the max y displacement given initial velocity and degree of projectile motion.

Kinematic Formula for Finding the Maximum X Displacement of a Projectile Shot From A Castle

RNGCSTL

This program is used for finding the max x displacement given degree of projectile motion, y position displacement, and initial velocity of projectile.

Centripetal Motion Formula for Finding the Gravity Field Strength of a Planet

GFSTR

This program is used for finding the gravity field of a planet given the mass of the planet and radius of the planet.

Centripetal Motion Formula for Finding Tension, Centripetal Force, and Period of a Swinging Mass

SWINGFC

This program is used for finding the tension force and centripetal force of a swing at an angel given length of string, angle of String, and mass.
