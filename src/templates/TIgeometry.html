Distance Formula for Finding Distance Between Two Points

DISTANCE

This programs is used for finding the distance between two points on a standard coordinate plane given the x,y coordinates of the known points.

Midpoint Formula for Finding the Midpoint Between Two Points

MIDPT

This programs is used for finding the midpoint between two points (point that is equidistant from the 2 points) on a standard coordinate plane given the x,y coordinates of the known points.

Quadractic Formula for Finding the Zeroes of a Quadractic Binomial

QDRACTIC

This programs is used for finding the zeroes of a quadractic graph on a standard coordinate plane given the quadractic expression.

This repository contains many programs to solve problems at the high school and college level. I developed these programs as another way to help me understand derivations. I solved each and every derivation by hand before I coded the programs in TI-BASIC. I suggest you or your students do the same before downloading the program. These are meant to aid in problem solving, rather than replace the concepts. I originally developed these programs on a TI-84 Plus calculator. In addition, they can be transferred to the TI-83 CE and TI-84 CE. I'm sure there are other calculators which can download the program as well, but these are the ones I know for sure will work.
