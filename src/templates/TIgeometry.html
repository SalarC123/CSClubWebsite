Geometry

Distance Formula for Finding Distance Between Two Points

DISTANCE

This program is used for finding the distance between two points on a standard coordinate plane given the x,y coordinates of the known points.

Midpoint Formula for Finding the Midpoint Between Two Points

MIDPT

This program is used for finding the midpoint between two points (point that is equidistant from the 2 points) on a standard coordinate plane given the x,y coordinates of the known points.

Quadractic Formula for Finding the Zeroes of a Quadractic Binomial

QDRACTIC

This program is used for finding the zeroes of a quadractic graph on a standard coordinate plane given the quadractic expression.
